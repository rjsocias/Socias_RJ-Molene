Experience Raw Power With Zx25r

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-25R is a 249 cc Ninja series sport bike introduced by the Japanese motorcycle manufacturer Kawasaki since 2020.

About ZX-25R

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-25R is a high-revving inline-4 sportbike with a liquid-cooled 249.8cc engine, producing 51 hp and 22.9 Nm of torque, featuring features like dual-channel ABS, KTRC, and two selectable riding modes.

Engine:

  • Type: Inline 4-cylinder, 4-stroke, 16-valve, liquid-cooled DOHC
  • Displacement: 249.8 cc
  • Maximum Power: 51 hp
  • Maximum Torque: 22.9 Nm
  • Bore x Stroke: 50 mm x 31.8 mm
  • Compression Ratio: 12.5:1
  • Fuel Injection: Yes
  • Exhaust Pipes: 4 into 1
  • RPM at Maximum Torque: 14500 rpm
  • RPM at Maximum Power: 15500 rpm
